Top Attractions in Hawaii

Attraction Type Description
Honolulu Zoo Paid Honolulu Zoo is the only zoo in the US that originated in a King's grant of royal lands.
Dole Plantation (Pineapple Place) Paid Originally operated as a fruit stand beginning in 1950, Dole Plantation opened to the public as Hawaii’s “Pineapple Experience” in 1989.
Diamond Head Paid Historic volcanic crater hike with panoramic views. $5 fee.
Waikiki Aquarium Paid Learn about Hawaiian marine life. Entry ticket required.
Hula Show at Kuhio Beach Free Watching a hula show is an absolute must-do for families, offering a chance to witness the grace and storytelling of this ancient Hawaiian art form.
Hawaii Symphony Orchestra Paid The Hawai‘i Symphony Orchestra is committed to entertaining, comforting, and inspiring through the power of music.
USS Arizona Memorial (Pearl Harbor) Free Historic site honoring those lost in WWII. Entry is free with reservation.
Iolani Palace Paid The only royal palace in the U.S. Guided tours available.
Ala Moana Beach Park Free Local favorite beach and park area near Waikiki.
Bishop Museum Paid Explore Hawaiian history, culture, and science exhibits.
